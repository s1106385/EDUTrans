Taking into account of the location of EdUHK, EdU Trans insist on providing transportation information between EdUHK facilities and destinations for EdUHK students and easing their schedule arrangments.
Operation of EdU Trans is a non-profit behavior, by providing maximal transport information to EdUHK students with limited resources unconditionally.
